The FRACTALS Research Group bridges the gap between the cosmos and life sciences. By leveraging the power of Computational Intelligence and High-Performance Computing, we navigate the intricacies of Complex Systems to drive innovation in Bio-Medicine, Therapeutics, and Astrophysics through fair, responsible, and interpretable algorithmic frameworks.

Our Mission: Decoding Complexity

At the core of FRACTALS lies the study of Complex Systems. We believe that the fundamental laws governing the universe, from the stochastic nature of cosmic rays to the non-linear dynamics of biological networks, share a common language that can be mastered through Computational Intelligence.

Our mission is to harness this complexity. By developing Fair and Responsible Algorithms, we provide the tools to navigate high-dimensional search spaces without compromising scientific rigor. We uncover the structural patterns of Life-Sciences and Astrophysics, ensuring that every discovery is grounded in the interpretable logic of Evolutionary Computation and Swarm Intelligence.

"At FRACTALS, Complex Systems are the core, Computational Intelligence is the method, and HPC is the engine. We decode the non-linear logic of nature to ensure algorithmic transparency."

Research Pillars

Computational Intelligence

Advancing Swarm Intelligence, Evolutionary Algorithms, and Fuzzy Logic to solve nature-inspired optimization problems.

Biological Systems

Mastering the non-linear dynamics governing life, from cellular networks to systemic health interactions.

Medicine

Developing advanced clinical diagnostics and precision healthcare models to translate data into life-saving outcomes.

Therapeutics, Drug Discovery and Design

Bridging Drug Discovery and Design through computational modeling to identify precision medicine solutions.

High Performance Computing (HPC)

Leveraging massive computational power to push the boundaries of data processing and simulation at scale.

GPU-Powered Simulations

Accelerating massive scientific workloads using CUDA-driven architectures for real-time complex systems modeling.

Astrophysics

Decoding the silent language of Cosmic Rays, turning chaotic high-energy data into physical insights about our universe.

Optimization Problems

Solving complex mathematical and algorithmic bottlenecks that govern both natural and artificial systems.

Interpretable AI

Opening the black box of complex models to provide human-understandable insights, essential for high-stakes scientific validation.

Fairness & Digital Ethics

Mitigating dataset and algorithmic bias to ensure equitable outcomes while aligning computational progress with robust legal, ethical, and societal standards.
