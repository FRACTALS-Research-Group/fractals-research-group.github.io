The FRACTALS Research Group masters the bridge between the cosmos and life. Operating through the lens of Complex Systems, we harness the peak of High-Performance Computing to drive innovation in Bio-Medicine, Therapeutics and Astrophysics, solving intricate problems with fair and interpretable intelligence.

Our Mission: Decoding Complexity

At the core of FRACTALS lies the study of Complex Systems. We believe that the fundamental laws governing the universe, from the stochastic nature of cosmic rays to the non-linear dynamics of biological networks, share a common mathematical language.

Our mission is to master this complexity. By developing Fair and Responsible Algorithms, we provide the tools to navigate these intricate systems without compromising scientific rigor. We don't just process data; we uncover the structural patterns of Astrophysics and Life-Sciences, ensuring that every discovery in Therapeutics and Medicine is grounded in the interpretable logic of complex interactions.

"At FRACTALS, Complex Systems are the core, and HPC is our engine. We decode the non-linear logic of life and space, ensuring that every algorithmic discovery is as fair and transparent as the laws of nature themselves."

Research Pillars

Biological Systems

Mastering the non-linear dynamics governing life, from cellular networks to systemic health interactions.

Medicine

Developing advanced clinical diagnostics and precision healthcare models to translate data into life-saving outcomes.

Therapeutics

Exploring new frontiers in pharmacological treatments through the lens of interpretable algorithmic frameworks.

Drug Discovery & Design

Disrupting traditional pipelines to identify candidates and molecular targets faster and more efficiently.

Astrophysics

Decoding the silent language of Cosmic Rays, turning chaotic high-energy data into physical insights about our universe.

High Performance Computing (HPC)

Leveraging massive computational power to push the boundaries of data processing and simulation at scale.

Optimization Problems

Solving complex mathematical and algorithmic bottlenecks that govern both natural phenomena and artificial systems.

Fair and Interpretable AI

Ensuring algorithmic transparency and eliminating bias to create trustworthy AI for scientific and societal progress.
